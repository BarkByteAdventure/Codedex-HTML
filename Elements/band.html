Silbermond

Silbermond is a German rock band formed in 2000 in Bautzen, Saxony. Known for their heartfelt lyrics and energetic performances, Silbermond has garnered widespread acclaim across German-speaking countries. With hits like "Symphonie" and "Das Beste," they've solidified their place as one of Germany's most beloved contemporary musical acts.
